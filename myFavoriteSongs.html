Music: My ability to fully express my culture

Music is one of the most important things in my life. It not only has the power to lift my spirts, but also allows me to connect with my culture. I am Dominican and Puerto Rican who never learned Spanish growing up. However, music became my gateway to learning the langauge in a fun way! It also allows me to have a better connection with my family.

The 3 Most Important Songs in My Life

  1. "Visa Para Un Sue√±o (Visa For a Dream)" by Juan Luis Guerra
    • This is the most important song in my life. It is a song that represents my father's experience in immigrating to the United States from the Dominican Republic. It is a song that highlights the struggles and dreams that many immigrants hold in their lives.
  2. "Mi Mujer Me Gobierna (My Woman Governs Me)" by La Banda Gorda
    • This is one of my favorite merengue songs as the tune is so catchy! I love the rhythm of the song, and I am reminded of the wonderful memories of my father and I dancing to this song.
  3. "Canalla (Scoundrel)" by Romeo Santos & El Chaval de la Bachata
    • This is song reminds me of my mother. This is one of her favorite songs, and ever time I listen to the song, I am reminded of my parents dancing to this song! It is the true representation of how music and bridge different cultures.
